A Homeless Management Information System solution in the Washington, D.C. area


Capitol HMIS seeks to provide an effective yet simple database management system to homeless shelters in Washington, D.C. and the surrounding areas. By coordinating services and information between shelters, Capitol HMIS offers an organized approach to homeless services.

Advantages:

  • Provides better reliability compared to existing systems
  • Includes a sleek and understandable user interface

Implemented Functions:

  • Search clients, case managers, shelter information, shelter stays, and services performed
  • Insert a new client, a new shelter, a shelter stay occurrence, and services rendered

The software facilitates data access by searching uniquely identifying information, such as a client’s SSN, an employee’s ID, a shelter’s number, or a combination of these attributes. In some instances, the user will be able to search clients and case managers by their last name. Capitol HMIS also has the ability to insert new, and update existing, clients, shelters, shelter stay instances, and service instances based on the user’s input. By using the Capitol HMIS database, case managers will be more equipped to handle the administrative tasks of homeless services.
